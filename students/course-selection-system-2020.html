Course Selection System 2020


Course Selection System - Online Portal (The portal will be online from 5 Feb 2020, 5pm to 8 Feb 2020, 12pm)


IMPORTANT:

  • Every student must register for a course of study using this Course Selection System. This is to ensure that you will be assigned a class during Matriculation Week.
  • You are only allowed to submit your registration ONCE.
  • You will NOT be able to change your options after you click 'Submit'. You can view and print your subject choices
  • You will have to proceed for paper registration if you change your mind or have made a mistake. So please choose carefully.
  • The portal will be online from 1700hrs on the 05 February 2020 to 1200hrs on 8 February 2020.
