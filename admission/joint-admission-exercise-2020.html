Joint Admission Exercise 2020


The Joint Admissions Exercise (JAE) is conducted every January by the Ministry of Education, Singapore (MOE) to allow Singapore-Cambridge GCE ‘O’ Level certificate holders to apply for admission to courses offered by junior colleges, Millennia Institute, polytechnics and the Institute of Technical Education (ITE).


4 February 2020

Through: Parents/Guardians

Dear JC1 students,

Welcome to Catholic Junior College

Congratulations on your posting to Catholic Junior College. I would like to extend a very warm welcome to you as you join the CJC family. My teachers and I look forward to guiding you in your educational journey to be Thinkers with a Mission and Leaders with a Heart.


ORIENTATION 2020 (5 to 10 February)

In order to help you settle in, we have specially organised an Orientation programme for all JC1 students joining the school. Your active participation in the Orientation activities will allow you to familiarise yourself with the college’s academic and co-curricular programmes, interact and bond with your seniors and other JC1 students. We look forward to seeing you at Orientation. Please take note of the following details:

·       Report to college punctually by 7.45 am on Wednesday, 5 Feb 2020 and gather at the Grandstand. You will be notified of your Orientation Group (OG) via SMS.

·       Be appropriately and neatly attired in your secondary school uniform, and bring along a set of Secondary School PE attire. Please also ensure that your hair is neat and tidy. Dyed hair and multiple earrings are not allowed.

·       The morning assembly begins at 7.45 am daily during Orientation. You are expected to be punctual for all activities and functions. Attendance will be marked at the beginning and end of each day.

·       The college will continue to implement daily temperature taking as a precautionary measure to monitor the health and well-being of all students and staff. You will be provided with a personal Oral Digital Thermometer (ODT) on 5 Feb and we will be conducting daily temperature taking after morning assembly. Should you feel unwell, please inform the teacher-in-charge immediately.

Students on Leave of Absence (LOA) after returning from Mainland China

In accordance with the precautionary measures implemented by the Ministry to manage the spread of the 2019-nCoV (Wuhan coronavirus), if you returned from mainland China on 22 Jan or later, you should not report to college on 5 Feb. You will be placed on Leave Of Absence (LOA) for 14 days, starting from the day after your arrival in Singapore. Please call (62524083) or email (Catholic_jc@moe.edu.sg) to inform us of your status as well as to confirm your place in CJC.

Selection of Subject Combination

The details of the subject combinations offered to all new students in 2020 are available on our college website (https://cjc.moe.edu.sg/admission/subject-combinations-2020). Please study the requirements carefully so that you can make an informed choice on the most appropriate subject combination that can cater to your interests and aspirations. As part of the orientation programme, we will be providing more information on subject combination and the selection process on 5 Feb to help you make an informed choice on your subject combination. 

Please select your subject combination in consultation with your parents. You need to register your subject combination choices on the CJC Course Selection System - Online Portal from 5.00 pm on 5 Feb (Wednesday) to 12.00 noon on 8 Feb (Saturday). More information will be made available on our website (https://cjc.moe.edu.sg/students/course-selection-system-2020).

Sale of College Attire and Scientific Calculator

The college uniform is an expression of your belonging and pride to the college. The sale of our uniform, Orientation t-shirt and P.E. attire will take place in college during Orientation. The House t- shirt and dress-down polo t-shirt can be purchased from the college bookshop after Orientation. More details can be found in Annex A.

If you are taking Mathematics and Science subjects, you are expected to have a Graphing Calculator, which may be purchased at a promotional price during Orientation (see Annex B).

MATRICULATION (11 to 21 February)

Matriculation for all JC1 students will commence on 11 February. During this period, you will attend the various lessons in your assigned classes. Further details on Matriculation will be communicated directly to you after the classes are formed.

StrengthsFinder Training

You will undergo a StrengthsFinder workshop during Matriculation, as part of the Student Development Curriculum in CJC, to help you understand yourself better and to build on your strengths. The college will subsidise the training and you are required to pay $20. More details on payment will be given later.

Should you have further queries, please contact the General Office at 6252 4083.

We look forward to having you join the college and may God bless you and your family as you embark on an exciting journey of learning and discovery.

Yours sincerely,

Mrs. Phyllis Lim

Principal
