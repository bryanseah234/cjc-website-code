Highlights

Mother Tongue Languages Oral Examinations on 10 July to be rescheduled

The GCE A-Level Mother Tongue Languages oral examinations on 10 July will be rescheduled due to Polling Day falling on the same date.

Thinker with a Mission, Leader with a Heart series

This series features students who exemplify the college's mission through their service to the community. We are proud of Nivetha's (Class of 2019) and Dawn Chueh's (Class of 2015) commitment to serve in these difficult times.

2020 Direct School Admission for JC1 2021 Intake

CJC invites applicants for direct admission to JC1 in 2021 from students who are academically able and who are outstanding in at least one area of CCA.

Adjustments to Academic Calendar and National Examinations for 2020

With the extension of the national Circuit Breaker measures until 1 June, adjustments were made to the academic calendar and MTL national examinations schedule for 2020.

Release of Project Work Results, 30 Apr

Results for H1 Project Work (8808) will be released to JC2 students on Thurs, 30 Apr, at 2 p.m. via Office 365.

Mandatory security measures for video conferencing platforms

The Ministry of Education puts in place compulsory security measures for video conferencing platforms to safeguard the teaching and learning environment for our students and teachers.

Full Home-Based Learning, 8 April to 4 May 2020

CJC will move to full Home-Based Learning from 8 April to 4 May 2020.

One day of Home-Based Learning a week from 1 Apr, 2020

CJC will introduce one day of Home-Based Learning a week from 1 April.

Schools will reopen on 23 Mar with enhanced precautionary measures

Schools will reopen for Term 2 on Monday, 23 Mar, as planned. There will be enhanced precautionary measures to keep our students and staff safe.

Advisory against all Non-essential Travel Abroad, 15 March 2020

Singaporeans are advised to defer all non-essential travel abroad to reduce the risk of contracting COVID-19.

Travel Declaration for March Term Break 2020

Parents/Guardians of all CJCians must make their travel declarations for the March term break using the Parents Gateway app by 11 March 2020.

Important Information for the Release of A-Level Results, 21 Feb 2020

To all CJCians from the Class of 2019 who will be receiving their A-Level results this year, please read this notification and ensure that you can access your results on 21 Feb.

COVID-19 Advisory (as at 10 Feb 2020)

With DORSCON Orange, added measures are in place to keep the CJC community safe and healthy.
