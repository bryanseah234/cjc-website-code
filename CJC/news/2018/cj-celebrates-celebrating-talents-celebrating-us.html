CJ Celebrates! Celebrating Talents, Celebrating Us

‘Celebrating Talents, Celebrating Us’ was indeed a celebration of our performing arts talents at our highly anticipated annual CJ Celebrates concert. Held this year on 29 June, this concert was a culmination of all the performing arts groups’ blood, sweat and tears over the months as they prepared for the big stage.

CJ Celebrates opened with a video filmed and directed by our very own Film, Sound and Video (FSV) Society. Thereafter, a wonderful repertoire from our Guzheng, Music Ministry, English Drama, Dance, Choir, Guitar and Symphonic Band ensued. What made this year’s celebration sweeter was the English Drama’s achievement of a Certificate of Distinction for their piece at the SYF competition this year.

Celebrating together with the CJ community was also a group of elderly from St Luke’s Eldercare whom CJC is collaborating with for our JC1 Local Service Learning Programme and fundraising efforts this year. Their presence highlights the importance CJC places on community outreach where we develop CJCians to be Thinkers with a Mission and Leaders with a Heart.
