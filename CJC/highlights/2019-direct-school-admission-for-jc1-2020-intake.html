2019 Direct School Admission for JC1 2020 Intake

2019 Direct School Admission - Junior Colleges (DSA-JC) Exercise For Admission To JC1 In 2020

CJC invites applications for direct admission to JC1 in 2020 from students who are academically able and who are outstanding in at least one area of CCA. Eligible applicants must sit for the GCE O-Levels in 2019. Successful applicants may be considered for one of the 10 FLAME scholarships available for significant sportspersons in 2020.

To learn more about Direct Admission Exercise, please visit the Ministry of Education website.

Admission Criteria

Evidence of the following must be provided with each application:

·       Good or reasonable Sec 3 & 4 results

·       Good CCA Records

·       Relevant CCA Achievements

·       Leadership qualities &/or service to the wider community

·       Teacher’s recommendation

We are looking for students who are outstanding in the following areas:

Performing Arts

Selection Criteria

·       Choir

·       Dance

·       Drama

·       Guzheng

·       Guitar

·       Symphonic band

·       Achievement in SYF presentations, &/or

·       Representation in international/national competitions, &/or

·       Achievement in music/dance exams, &/or

·       Interviews &/or auditions

Physical Sports

Selection Criteria

·       Fencing

·       Golf

·       Judo

·       Shooting

·       Track & Field / Cross Country

·       Badminton

·       Canoeing

·       Football

·       Floorball

·       Netball

·       Rugby

·       Basketball

·       Swimming

·       Ten-pin Bowling

·       Tennis

·       Volleyball

·       Achievement in National Schools/ international competitions, &/or

·       Representation at National or zonal schools &/or

·       Interviews &/or trials

Others areas of expertise / talent

Selection Criteria

·       Debates & Public Speaking (Oratorical Society)

·       Representation at National or zonal schools competition

·       Record of achievement with CCA &/or

·       Interviews &/or auditions

·       Student Leadership

·       Holding a key school-wide leadership position in Secondary 3 and 4 (e.g. Exco member of Student Council / Prefectorial Board or of similar leadership equivalent)

·       Record of strong leadership capabilities &/or

·       Interviews

Applicants are to focus on only one area of expertise / talent / interest in their application.

Application Procedures

1.  To apply, please register using the online form at http://simscjc.learnaholic.com/JC1DSA 

After you have completed the online registration, please print and submit the application form together with all supporting documents to CJC General Office. Official operating hours are from 9 am to 4 pm on weekdays.

For DSA-JC (Phase 1)
To allow sufficient time for processing, it is strongly recommended that all application forms are to reach Catholic Junior College, 129 Whitley Road, S(297822) by 4pm, Friday, 5 July 2019. 

Applications forms submitted after 5 July 2019 will be processed in Phase 2. 

For DSA-JC (Phase 2)
To allow sufficient time for processing, it is strongly recommended that all application forms are to reach Catholic Junior College, 129 Whitley Road, S(297822) by 1pm, Thursday, 8 August 2019. 

2.  The following supporting documents are required:

• Certified copy of Sec 3 End-of-Year exam results
• Certified copy of Sec 4 Mid-Year exam results
• Certified copy of CCA & VIA records 
• Teacher’s recommendation

3.  Incomplete submission will not be considered.

Trial/Audition Process

For DSA-JC (Phase 1),

·       Short-listed applicants will be invited for auditions/trials from 27 May 2019 onwards.

·       Students are reminded to be punctual for trials/auditions and are required to be in their Secondary School sports attire or school uniform. 

·       Having granted a trial/audition does not mean that the DSA-JC application has been successful.

For DSA-JC (Phase 2),

·       Short-listed applicants will be invited for auditions/trials from 22 July 2019 onwards.

·       Students are reminded to be punctual for trials/auditions and are required to be in their Secondary School sports attire or school uniform. 

·       Having granted a trial/audition does not mean that the DSA-JC application has been successful.

Selection Process

For DSA-JC (Phase 1),

·       Only successful candidates will be invited for a short interview between 12 July and 1 August 2019.

·       Final decision will be made by the Interview Panel after the interview.

·       Successful applicants will be issued with a provisional letter of offer.

·       Applicant’s acceptance of DSA offer must reach CJC by 3 pm, Friday, 2 August 2019.

For DSA-JC (Phase 2),

·       Only successful candidates will be invited for a short interview between 6 and 22 August 2019.

·       Final decision will be made by the Interview Panel after the interview.

·       Successful applicants will be issued with a provisional letter of offer.

·       Applicant’s acceptance of DSA offer must reach CJC by 3 pm, Thursday, 29 August 2019.
